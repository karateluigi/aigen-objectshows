Object Shows

An object show is a type of animated series in which everyday objects compete in challenges to win a prize. These shows are often created using 2D animation software and can be found on YouTube and other online platforms. Object shows are known for their unique characters, creative challenges, and humorous commentary.

Battle for Dream Island

Battle for Dream Island (BFDI) is a popular object show created by Michael and Cary Huang. The show follows a group of inanimate objects as they compete in various challenges for a chance to win "dream island". The show has multiple seasons, including BFDIA, IDFB, and BFB, each with its own unique set of challenges and characters. You can watch BFDI on the channel, jacknjellify.

Inanimate Insanity

Inanimate Insanity is another popular object show created by Adam Katz. The show follows a group of 20 inanimate objects as they compete in challenges for a chance to win a grand prize of one million dollars. The show has three seasons, each with its own unique set of challenges and characters. You can watch Inanimate Insanity on the channel, AnimationEpic.
